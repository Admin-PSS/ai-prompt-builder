# 📸 AI Image Editing Prompt Guide (Complete)

This document covers:
- Background editing
- Clothing changes
- Body adjustments
- Face realism
- Age editing
- Makeup & expression
- Pose control
- Frame expansion (face → full body)
- Modular prompt system

---

# 🔑 Core Principles

1. Preserve Identity
   - Keep the same person
   - Do not change facial features unless explicitly required

2. Be Specific
   - Avoid vague prompts like “make it better”

3. Keep Changes Subtle
   - Use: slightly, naturally, balanced

4. Maintain Realism
   - Natural lighting
   - Correct anatomy
   - Realistic textures

---

# 🧠 GENERAL PROMPT STRUCTURE

[Identity preservation] + [What to change] + [Details] + [Realism constraints]

---

# 🖼️ BACKGROUND EDITING

Keep the same person exactly. Replace the background with a modern office environment with natural daylight. Maintain facial features, pose, and lighting. Ensure realistic shadows and depth.

---

# 👗 CLOTHING CHANGE

Keep the same person. Replace the outfit with an elegant red satin dress. Ensure natural fabric folds and realistic lighting. Maintain pose and proportions.

---

# 🧍 BODY ADJUSTMENTS

Keep the same person. Slightly adjust body proportions to appear taller while maintaining natural anatomy and pose.

---

# 👶 AGE EDITING

Keep the same person. Make the face appear about 10 years younger by reducing wrinkles and smoothing skin slightly. Maintain natural texture and expression.

---

# 😊 FACE REALISM

Keep the same person. Enhance facial realism with natural skin texture, pores, and subtle imperfections. Avoid artificial smoothing.

---

# 💄 MAKEUP & EXPRESSION

Keep the same person. Apply elegant evening makeup with soft smoky eyes and deep red lipstick. Adjust expression to confident with a subtle smile. Maintain realistic skin texture.

---

# 🧍 POSE CONTROL

Keep the same person. Stand upright with relaxed shoulders. Place one hand on the hip and the other at the side. Maintain natural posture and balance.

---

# 📚 BODY PART TERMINOLOGY

Face: eyes, eyebrows, nose, lips, chin, jawline, cheeks  
Upper Body: shoulders, chest, waist, back  
Arms: upper arms, elbows, forearms, wrists, hands, fingers  
Lower Body: hips, thighs, knees, calves, ankles, feet  

---

# 🔍 FRAME EXPANSION

Face → Upper Body:
Keep the same person. Expand to show shoulders, chest, and arms. Maintain correct proportions and lighting.

Upper Body → Full Body:
Extend to full body including legs and feet. Maintain realistic anatomy and perspective.

---
