👉 Image A (person) + Image B (person)

There are actually different meanings of “combine”, and your prompt must clarify which one you want.

🧠 First: Decide the Goal (VERY IMPORTANT)

When combining two people, you usually want one of these:

1. 🧍‍♂️🧍‍♀️ Put both people in one image (side by side)
2. 🔄 Face swap (A ↔ B)
3. 🧘 Apply Person B’s pose to Person A
4. 👗 Dress Person A in Person B’s clothes
5. 🤝 Put both people together with active interaction
6. 🌆 Place Person A into Person B’s background/setting
7. 🎭 Hybrid / blended face (advanced)

👉 Each requires a different prompt style

🔑 Universal Rule

Always define roles clearly:

Image A = Person A
Image B = Person B

🧩 1. Put Both People Together (Most Common)
✅ Prompt
Use Image A and Image B as two separate individuals.

Place both people together in the same scene, standing naturally side by side.

Preserve the identity, facial features, and proportions of both persons.

Ensure consistent lighting, perspective, and scale between the two subjects.

Adjust shadows and positioning so both appear naturally in the same environment.

Make the result photorealistic and seamless.
💡 Add Position Control
Position Person A on the left and Person B on the right with natural spacing.
🧩 2. Face Swap (A ↔ B)
✅ Prompt
Use Image A as the base image.

Replace the face in Image A with the face from Image B.

Ensure the facial features from Image B are accurately transferred while maintaining natural alignment with the body in Image A.

Match skin tone, lighting, and perspective for realistic blending.

Preserve natural proportions and avoid distortion.
🧩 3. Person A in Pose of Person B
✅ Prompt
Use Image A as the base image and preserve the identity of the person.

Use Image B as a reference for body pose.

Apply the pose from Image B to the person in Image A,
including arm and leg positioning.

Maintain natural anatomy, balance, and realistic proportions.

Ensure the face and identity from Image A remain unchanged.
🧩 4. Person A Wearing Clothes from Person B
✅ Prompt
Use Image A as the base image and keep the same person.

Use Image B as a reference for clothing.

Replace the outfit in Image A with the clothing from Image B.

Ensure realistic fabric texture, proper fit, and natural folds.

Maintain the identity, pose, and proportions of the person in Image A.
🧩 5. Both People Together — With Interaction
*Use this instead of #1 when you want them actively engaged, not just coexisting.*
✅ Prompt
Use Image A and Image B as two separate individuals.

Place both people in the same environment, interacting naturally (e.g., standing together, talking, or walking).

Preserve the identity and facial features of both individuals.

Ensure correct scale, perspective, and lighting consistency.

Add natural shadows and positioning so both subjects appear grounded and realistic.

Avoid any distortion or artificial appearance.
🧩 6. Hybrid / Blended Face (Advanced)

⚠️ This is tricky and less stable — results are less predictable than other methods.

✅ Prompt
Create a blended face combining features from Image A and Image B.

Maintain a natural and realistic appearance with balanced proportions.

Ensure smooth integration of facial features without distortion.

Keep lighting and skin tone consistent.
🧩 7. Place Person A into Person B's Background / Setting
✅ Prompt
Use Image A as the subject.

Use Image B as the background/environment reference only — do not include the person from Image B.

Place the person from Image A into the setting from Image B.

Ensure consistent lighting, perspective, and scale between the subject and the new environment.

Adjust shadows and reflections so the person appears naturally integrated into the scene.

⚠️ Common Mistakes
❌ “Combine these two people”

👉 Too vague → unpredictable

❌ No role definition

👉 Model doesn’t know who controls what

❌ No scale/lighting instructions

👉 Results look fake

💡 Pro Tips
1. Always Separate Instructions

👉 Good:

A = identity
B = pose

👉 Bad:

“mix them”
2. Control Interaction

Add:

Ensure natural spacing and interaction between both subjects
🔥 Strong “All-in-One” Prompt (Two People Together)
Use Image A and Image B as two separate individuals.

Place both people together in the same scene, standing naturally side by side.

Preserve the identity, facial features, and proportions of both persons.

Ensure consistent lighting, perspective, and scale between them.

Add natural shadows and grounding so both appear realistically integrated.

Avoid distortion or artificial appearance.
