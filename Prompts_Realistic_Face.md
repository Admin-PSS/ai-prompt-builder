Key Conditions for Realistic Face Prompts
1. Lock Identity (critical)

Always say:

same person
do not change facial features
preserve identity
2. Focus on Skin Realism (not “beauty”)

Use:

natural skin texture
visible pores
subtle imperfections
fine details

👉 Avoid words like “perfect”, “flawless” (they cause fake results)

3. Improve Lighting & Detail

Realism comes from:

natural lighting
soft shadows
accurate highlights
4. Avoid Over-Smoothing

Explicitly say:

no over-smoothing
no plastic skin
no artificial look
5. Keep Everything Else Unchanged
same pose
same expression
same background
same camera angle
🧠 Prompt Structure

[Identity lock] + [realism enhancement] + [skin details] + [lighting] + [constraints]

✅ Example Prompts
1. Simple & Effective

“Keep the same person exactly as in the original image. Do not change facial features or identity. Enhance the face to look more natural and realistic by improving skin texture and fine details. Maintain the same pose, expression, and background. Avoid any artificial or overly smooth appearance.”

2. Skin Texture Focus

“Preserve the same person with identical facial features. Improve facial realism by adding natural skin texture, visible pores, and subtle imperfections. Keep lighting consistent and realistic. Do not alter pose, expression, or identity.”

3. High Realism / Photographic Style

“Keep the same person exactly. Do not modify facial structure or identity. Enhance the face to be highly photorealistic with natural skin texture, fine details, and realistic lighting. Avoid over-smoothing or artificial effects. Maintain the same pose, expression, and background.”

4. DSLR / Camera Realism

“Keep the same person unchanged. Improve facial realism using natural skin texture, accurate lighting, and fine detail as if captured with a DSLR camera. Maintain original pose, expression, and identity. Avoid any plastic or AI-generated look.”

5. Ultra-Controlled Version

“Keep the same person exactly as in the original image. Do not change facial features, proportions, or identity. Enhance realism by refining skin texture, adding subtle imperfections, and improving natural lighting and shadows. Avoid over-smoothing, artificial glow, or unrealistic perfection. Keep everything else unchanged.”

❌ Weak Prompt Example

“Make face better and realistic”

🔴 Problems:

vague
no identity protection
no detail → unpredictable
💡 Pro Tips
If face changes → add:
“preserve exact facial structure and proportions”
If skin looks fake → add:
“natural pores, subtle imperfections”
If too smooth → add:
“no beauty filter, no airbrushing”
If still looks AI → add:
“photorealistic, DSLR, natural lighting, depth of field”
🔥 Strong “All-in-One” Prompt

Enhance the face to look more natural and photorealistic by improving skin texture, adding subtle imperfections, and refining lighting and shadows.
