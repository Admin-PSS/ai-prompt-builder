Key Conditions for “Younger Age” Prompts
1. Lock Identity (critical)

Always include:

same person
do not change facial structure
preserve identity
2. Specify “Subtle De-Aging”

Use phrases like:

“approximately 10 years younger”
“slightly more youthful appearance”

👉 Avoid “make very young” (causes unrealistic changes)

3. Define What Changes (and what doesn’t)

You want:

smoother skin (not plastic)
reduced wrinkles
slightly firmer facial contours
natural youthful glow

You DON’T want:

different face shape
different eyes/nose/mouth
4. Keep Everything Else Constant
same pose
same expression
same lighting
same background
5. Add Realism Constraints
natural skin texture (important!)
subtle imperfections
realistic lighting
🧠 Prompt Structure

[Identity lock] + [age change] + [specific facial adjustments] + [keep everything else same] + [realism constraints]

✅ Example Prompts
1. Simple & Effective

“Keep the same person exactly as in the original image. Do not change facial features or identity. Make the person look approximately 10 years younger with slightly smoother skin and reduced wrinkles. Maintain the same expression, pose, lighting, and background. Ensure the result is natural and photorealistic.”

2. More Detailed (Better Control)

“Preserve the same person with identical facial structure and identity. Adjust the appearance to look about 10 years younger by subtly smoothing skin, reducing fine lines, and slightly improving skin firmness. Keep the same pose, expression, and lighting. Maintain natural skin texture and avoid any artificial or overly smooth look.”

3. High Realism / Professional

“Keep the same person exactly. Do not alter facial structure, proportions, or identity. Apply a subtle de-aging effect of about 10 years by reducing wrinkles, slightly tightening facial contours, and enhancing natural skin tone. Maintain original pose, expression, and lighting. Ensure photorealistic skin texture with natural imperfections.”

4. Ultra-Controlled Version

“Keep the same person unchanged. Make them appear approximately 10 years younger while preserving facial identity and structure. Reduce wrinkles and fine lines, slightly smooth skin, and subtly enhance facial firmness. Do not change eyes, nose, or mouth. Maintain the same pose, expression, lighting, and background. Ensure realistic skin texture and avoid over-smoothing.”

❌ Weak Prompt Example

“Make this person younger”

🔴 Problems:

no identity protection
no age range
no realism guidance
💡 Pro Tips
If face changes → add:
“preserve exact facial structure and proportions”
If skin looks fake → add:
“natural skin texture, not overly smooth”
If result looks like a different person:
repeat identity constraint twice
