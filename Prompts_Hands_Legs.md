🧍‍♂️ 1. How to Prompt for Hands & Legs Position
🔑 Key Rules
Be specific (not “nice pose”)
Use direction + location + action
Keep natural anatomy
✋ Hands Position Examples
Neutral / Relaxed

“Keep the same person. Position the arms naturally at the sides with relaxed hands.”

Hands on Hips

“Place both hands on the hips with elbows slightly angled outward. Maintain a natural posture.”

One Hand Gesture

“Position one hand gently touching the chin while the other arm rests naturally.”

Crossed Arms

“Cross the arms comfortably in front of the chest with relaxed shoulders.”

Holding Object

“Position the right hand holding a phone naturally, with relaxed fingers.”

🦵 Legs Position Examples
Standing Natural

“Keep both legs straight with a natural stance, feet slightly apart.”

One Leg Relaxed

“Shift weight to one leg while the other leg is slightly bent for a relaxed pose.”

Sitting Pose

“Position the person sitting with legs crossed at the knees in a natural way.”

Walking Style

“Create a natural walking pose with one leg stepping forward and arms slightly swinging.”

🧍 Full Pose Example

“Keep the same person exactly. Position the body standing upright with relaxed shoulders. One hand resting on the hip and the other hanging naturally. Shift weight slightly to one leg, with the other leg relaxed. Maintain natural anatomy and balance.”

🧠 2. Best Prompt Structure for Body Position

Use this formula:

[Identity lock] + [body posture] + [arms/hands] + [legs/feet] + [balance + realism]

🔥 Strong Example

“Keep the same person exactly. Position the body in a relaxed standing pose with straight posture. Place the right hand on the hip and the left arm resting naturally. Shift weight slightly to one leg, with the other leg slightly bent. Ensure natural anatomy, balance, and realistic posture.”

⚠️ Common Mistakes

❌ “Make pose better”
❌ “Fix hands”
❌ “natural pose”

👉 Too vague → weird hands/legs

“natural finger positioning, anatomically correct hands”
If pose looks stiff → add:
“relaxed posture, natural body balance”
If body distorts → add:
“maintain correct proportions and skeletal structure”
