Extend Key Conditions (Very Important)
1. Lock Identity + Existing Image
keep the same person
preserve face exactly
do not alter original area
2. Specify Expansion Direction

Be clear:

extend from face → upper body
extend to full body
3. Control Body Continuity

To avoid weird anatomy:

consistent proportions
natural alignment with existing face
realistic posture
4. Match Style & Lighting

Must match original:

lighting direction
color tone
camera perspective
🧠 Prompt Structure

[Identity lock] + [expand frame] + [what to add] + [body posture] + [consistency constraints]

✅ Example Prompts
🧍 1. Face → Upper Body

“Keep the same person exactly as in the original image. Do not change the face or identity. Extend the image to include the upper body (shoulders, chest, and arms). Ensure natural body proportions aligned with the face. Match lighting, skin tone, and perspective with the original image.”

🧍‍♂️ 2. Face → Half Body (Waist Up)

“Preserve the same person with identical facial features. Expand the image from the face to show the upper half of the body down to the waist. Add natural shoulders, torso, and arms with correct proportions. Maintain consistent lighting, perspective, and realism.”

🧍‍♀️ 3. Face → Full Body

“Keep the same person exactly as in the original image. Do not alter the face. Extend the image to a full-body view, including torso, legs, and feet. Ensure natural anatomy, correct proportions, and realistic posture. Match lighting, shadows, and camera angle with the original image.”

🧍 4. Upper Body → Full Body

“Keep the same person unchanged. Extend the existing upper-body image to show the full body. Add lower body, legs, and feet with realistic proportions and natural posture. Maintain consistent lighting, perspective, and style.”

🧍‍♂️ Add Pose Control (Very Useful)

You can combine with pose:

“Extend to full body with a relaxed standing pose, arms at the sides and weight slightly shifted to one leg.”

👗 Add Clothing (Optional but Recommended)

If not specified → random clothing

“Add a natural outfit consistent with the upper body, such as a casual shirt and jeans.”

❌ Weak Prompt Example

“Make it full body”

🔴 Problems:

no identity protection
no proportion control
no lighting match
💡 Pro Tips
If body looks disconnected:

Add:

“ensure seamless transition from face to body”

If proportions are wrong:

Add:

“maintain correct anatomical proportions and scale”

If style mismatch:

Add:

“match original image lighting, color tone, and perspective exactly”

If face changes:

Repeat:

“do not modify face or identity in any way”

🔥 Strong “All-in-One” Prompt

“Keep the same person exactly as in the original image. Do not change the face or identity. Extend the image to show the full body, ensuring a seamless transition from the existing face to the body. Maintain correct anatomical proportions, natural posture, and realistic alignment. Match lighting, shadows, color tone, and camera perspective with the original image. Avoid any distortion or inconsistency.”
