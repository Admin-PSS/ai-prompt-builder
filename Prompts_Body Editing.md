🔑 Key Conditions for Body Editing Prompts
1. Preserve Identity

Always include:

same person
do not change face or identity
2. Keep It Subtle & Proportional

For realism:

“slightly increase”
“naturally proportioned”
“anatomically realistic”

👉 Big changes = unnatural results

3. Use Neutral, Non-Sexual Language

Instead of sexual wording, use:

body proportions
silhouette
figure
chest / waist / hips (neutral terms)
4. Maintain Pose & Structure

Avoid distortions:

keep same pose
keep same camera angle
preserve body alignment
5. Add Realism Constraints
natural anatomy
realistic muscle/fat distribution
consistent lighting and shadows
🧠 Prompt Structure

[Identity lock] + [Body adjustment] + [Subtlety + proportions] + [Realism constraints] + [Pose preservation]

✅ Example Prompts
1. Slight Height Increase

“Keep the same person exactly as in the original image. Do not change the face or identity. Slightly increase the person’s height while maintaining natural body proportions. Keep the same pose and camera angle. Ensure the result looks anatomically realistic with correct lighting and perspective.”

2. Overall Body Proportion Adjustment

“Preserve the same person with identical facial features. Adjust the body proportions slightly to create a more balanced and natural silhouette. Maintain realistic anatomy, natural posture, and the same pose. Avoid any distortion.”

3. Slimmer or Fuller Figure

“Keep the same person unchanged. Slightly adjust the body to a more slim and toned physique while maintaining natural proportions. Preserve pose and lighting. Ensure realistic muscle and body structure.”

OR

“Keep the same person unchanged. Slightly enhance a fuller, natural body shape with balanced proportions. Maintain anatomical realism and original pose.”

4. Chest / Waist / Hip Adjustment (Neutral Tone)

“Keep the same person exactly. Make a subtle adjustment to the body proportions by slightly enhancing the chest and hip area while maintaining a natural and balanced silhouette. Do not alter the face or pose. Ensure realistic anatomy and smooth transitions.”

5. Posture + Proportion Improvement

“Preserve the same person and identity. Slightly improve posture and body proportions to appear taller and more balanced. Keep the same pose and camera angle. Ensure realistic anatomy and natural lighting.”

❌ Avoid Prompts Like

“Make her hotter with bigger sexy body parts”

🔴 Problems:

vague
sexualized wording
leads to unrealistic or rejected results
💡 Pro Tips
Use “slightly” or “subtle” → more realistic outputs
Add “anatomically correct” → prevents distortions
If stretching happens → add:
“maintain correct skeletal proportions”
If face changes → repeat:
“do not modify face or identity in any way”
🔥 Strong “All-in-One” Prompt

“Keep the same person exactly as in the original image. Do not change facial features or identity. Make a subtle adjustment to body proportions (e.g., slightly taller and more balanced figure) while maintaining natural anatomy. Preserve the same pose and camera angle. Ensure realistic lighting, shadows, and smooth, proportional transitions. Avoid any distortion or artificial appearance.”
