Core Conditions (Very Important)
1. Preserve Identity

Always include:

same person
do not change facial structure
keep natural features
2. Separate 3 Things Clearly

Your prompt should distinguish:

Makeup (color, intensity, style)
Facial features adjustment (e.g., lips slightly thinner/fuller)
Expression / emotion

👉 If you mix them → results become messy

3. Keep Adjustments Subtle

Use:

slightly
gently
natural-looking

Avoid:

extreme, exaggerated
4. Match Context (VERY IMPORTANT)

Makeup should match:

location (party, office, outdoor)
lighting (night, indoor, daylight)
vibe (elegant, casual, bold)
🧠 Prompt Structure

[Identity lock] + [Makeup details] + [Facial adjustments] + [Expression/emotion] + [Context/style] + [Realism constraints]

💄 PART 1: Makeup Prompts
Lip Color

“Apply natural-looking makeup with a soft matte red lip color. Keep it realistic with natural texture and no over-saturation.”

Light / Natural Makeup

“Apply subtle, natural makeup with light foundation, soft blush, and neutral lip color. Maintain realistic skin texture.”

Bold Makeup

“Apply elegant evening makeup with defined eyeliner, subtle shimmer eyeshadow, and deep red lipstick. Keep it refined and realistic.”

👄 PART 2: Lip Shape Adjustments (Neutral Tone)
Slightly Fuller Lips

“Keep the same person. Slightly enhance lip fullness while maintaining natural proportions and realistic texture.”

Slightly Thinner Lips

“Keep the same person. Subtly reduce lip fullness for a more refined and natural look. Maintain realistic proportions.”

Lip Definition

“Enhance lip definition with natural contouring while keeping realistic texture and proportions.”

👁️ PART 3: Eyes & Expression
Soft / Friendly Expression

“Adjust expression to a soft, friendly look with relaxed eyes and a gentle smile.”

Confident / Elegant

“Create a confident and elegant expression with slightly sharper eye focus and a subtle smile.”

Happy / Bright

“Adjust expression to a natural, happy look with bright eyes and a warm smile.”

Neutral / Professional

“Maintain a calm, neutral expression with relaxed facial features.”

🎉 PART 4: Makeup by Location / Event
1. Evening Party / Cocktail

“Keep the same person exactly. Apply elegant evening makeup with defined eyeliner, subtle shimmer eyeshadow, and deep-toned lipstick. Adjust expression to confident and slightly smiling. Ensure lighting matches an indoor evening party with warm tones. Maintain realistic skin texture and identity.”

2. Outdoor Garden Party

“Keep the same person unchanged. Apply light, fresh makeup with soft blush, natural lip color, and minimal eye makeup. Create a relaxed and happy expression. Match natural daylight and soft shadows. Ensure photorealistic skin texture.”

3. Night Club / Glam Look

“Keep the same person exactly. Apply bold glam makeup with smoky eyes, highlighted cheekbones, and glossy lips. Adjust expression to confident and slightly dramatic. Use low-light, colorful nightclub lighting while keeping realism.”

4. Formal Gala / Luxury Event

“Keep the same person unchanged. Apply sophisticated, high-end makeup with smooth foundation, elegant eye makeup, and rich lipstick tone. Expression should be poised and confident. Maintain photorealistic detail and lighting consistent with a formal indoor event.”

❌ Weak Prompt Example

“Make her makeup better and sexy”

🔴 Problems:

vague
not controllable
may distort identity
💡 Pro Tips
If makeup looks fake → add:
“natural skin texture, no heavy filters”
If identity changes → repeat:
“do not alter facial structure or identity”
If lips distort → add:
“maintain natural proportions”
Combine carefully:
makeup + expression + lighting must match
🔥 Strong “All-in-One” Prompt

“Keep the same person exactly as in the original image. Do not change facial features or identity. Apply [describe makeup in detail]. Make a subtle adjustment to [lip/eye feature if needed] while maintaining natural proportions. Adjust expression to [emotion]. Match the lighting and style to [event/location]. Ensure photorealistic skin texture, natural makeup blending, and avoid any artificial or exaggerated look.”
