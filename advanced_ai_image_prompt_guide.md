# 🧩 EXPANDED MODULES

## Identity Lock
Preserve identity, facial structure, skin tone, and natural expression.

## Frame Expansion
- Face → Upper Body
- Upper Body → Full Body
Maintain seamless transition and correct proportions.

## Clothing Module
Specify:
- Type (dress, suit, casual)
- Color
- Material (silk, cotton, denim)
- Fit (loose, fitted)
Ensure realistic folds and lighting.

## Makeup Module
Specify:
- Lip color
- Eye makeup
- Skin finish
Keep natural texture and avoid over-smoothing.

## Expression Module
Options:
- soft smile
- confident
- neutral
- happy
- elegant

## Background Module
Specify:
- Location (office, beach, café)
- Time (day, sunset, night)
- Style (modern, cinematic)

## Lighting Module
Options:
- soft daylight
- golden hour
- indoor warm light
- studio lighting

# 📚 BODY TERMINOLOGY

Face: eyes, eyebrows, nose, lips, chin, jawline, cheeks  
Upper Body: shoulders, chest, waist, back  
Arms: elbows, forearms, wrists, hands, fingers  
Lower Body: hips, thighs, knees, calves, ankles, feet
